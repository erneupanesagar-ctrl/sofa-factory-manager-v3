=== DATA FLOW ANALYSIS ===

1. SALES WORKFLOW:
   Customer → Order → Production → Sale → Payment → Cleaning Service
   
2. PURCHASE WORKFLOW:
   Supplier → Purchase → Raw Materials → Inventory
   
3. PRODUCTION WORKFLOW:
   Order → Raw Materials + Labour → Finished Product → Stock
   
4. FINANCIAL WORKFLOW:
   Sales (Income) + Cleaning (Income) - Purchases (Expense) - Labour (Expense) = Profit

5. INVENTORY WORKFLOW:
   Purchase → Raw Materials Stock
   Production → Finished Products Stock
   Sale → Finished Products Stock (Decrease)
